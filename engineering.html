Why I Choose Engineering

Turning curiosity into real-world solutions

🔊

Engineering

Engineering, for me, is the bridge between curiosity and impact. As a woman and a minority in a field where we are often underrepresented, I’ve learned that my perspective isn’t just a difference—it’s a strength. Navigating a male-dominated space has fueled my determination to lead and motivated me to build technology that is as inclusive as it is innovative. I choose engineering because it gives me the toolkit to turn complex challenges into real-world solutions, and because I want to be the representation that inspires the next generation of girls to realize they don't just belong in STEM—they are the future of it.

Learning through building 🔧

Whether I’m working with electronics, coding a prototype, or iterating on a website design, I get to learn by doing. Debugging and improving my work is where the growth happens.

Helping people matters ❤️

Engineering is powerful when it’s built with purpose. I’m motivated by projects that solve real problems, support learning, or make everyday experiences better.

Next: building more + bigger 🚀

I’m excited to keep experimenting, collaborating, and turning new ideas into real projects. The best part is—there’s always something new to learn.
